StreetHorizon drops you into a pulse-pounding open world of illegal street racing. Navigate rain-slicked highways, drift through neon-lit tunnels, and outrun your rivals across a sprawling urban landscape.

Powered by Unreal Engine 5, every car, every puddle, and every explosion is rendered with cinematic realism. Your mobile device becomes a console-grade racing machine.

⚡ Real Physics

UE5 Chaos Physics engine simulates real-world vehicle dynamics.

Open World

Explore a 20 km² city — race day or night across 8 districts.

Car Customization

Deep visual and performance tuning for over 50 licensed vehicles.

Real-time 16-player races with skill-based global matchmaking.

1. Information We Collect

StreetHorizon Studio ("we", "us", "our") collects information to provide and improve the game. We may collect:

  • Device identifiers and hardware information
  • Gameplay data, statistics, and session duration
  • In-app purchase history (processed securely via Google Play)
  • Crash reports and performance diagnostics
  • Voluntarily provided account data (username, email)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data exclusively to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve StreetHorizon
  • Provide multiplayer matchmaking and leaderboards
  • Detect and prevent cheating or fraudulent activity
  • Send optional game updates and announcements

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share anonymized, aggregated analytics with third-party services (e.g., Unreal Engine analytics, crash reporting). Any third-party integrations are bound by their own privacy policies.

4. Children's Privacy

StreetHorizon is rated for users 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us immediately at privacy@streethorizongame.com.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest. However, no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at privacy@streethorizongame.com. We will respond within 30 days.

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated via in-game notification. Continued use of the game constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
