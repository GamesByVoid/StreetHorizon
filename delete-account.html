Street Horizon Account Deletion

If you want to delete your Street Horizon account and all associated data, you can request account deletion by contacting us.

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to: ContactStreetHorizon@gmail.com
  2. Include your Player ID or username
  3. Write "Account Deletion Request" in the email subject

Data deletion

Once we receive your request, your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

This includes:

  • Player profile
  • Game progress
  • Stored account information

If you have any questions, contact us at ContactStreetHorizon@gmail.com.
